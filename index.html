About & Philosophy

AI Microapps Hub is built to demonstrate that small, single-purpose AI tools can be powerful and shareable. The goal: fast utility, low friction, and a platform where anyone can add a microapp.

Why free & browser-first?

  • Privacy: all demo features run locally — your text stays in the browser.
  • Cost-free: static hosting and heuristic tools mean zero monthly bills for you at early stages.
  • Fast: less latency, instant sharing, and easy iterations.
